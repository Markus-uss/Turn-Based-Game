Your wife has been dragged to the depths of hell by Hades.
Determined to bring her back, you make your way to the nine circles of hell.

Your journey begins in the 1st circle of Hell, there is danger ahead.
Charon, the ferryman of the River Styx, stands in his boat,
awaiting to take those who carry a ticket.
Maybe the shop nearby will have the fee of passage into the second circle.
